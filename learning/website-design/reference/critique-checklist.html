🎨 The 4-Lever Critique

  1. Hierarchy — eye lands where it should? One clear leader, everything else de-emphasized?
  2. Spacing — room to breathe? Related close, unrelated far? Nothing cramped?
  3. Typography — ≤2 typefaces, deliberate scale, 45–75 char lines, body text softened (not pure black)?
  4. Color — neutral base + ONE disciplined accent? Real text contrast?

🗣 Director's vocabulary

  • "Increase section padding / vertical rhythm"
  • "De-emphasize the secondary text — smaller, muted gray"
  • "Tighten the headline (letter-spacing, line-height)"
  • "Reserve the accent color for the primary CTA only"
  • "The hero needs one focal point, not three"
  • "This section drifts from the mockup — match its spacing/palette"
